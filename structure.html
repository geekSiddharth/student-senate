Internal Structure

The following are the internal positions in Student Senate:

  • Coordinator: Head of SS(Student Senate), SPOC(Single Point Of Contact) to Academic Department and UGC(Undergraduate Council), Student representative in Institute Council, member of UGC
  • Secretary: Responsible for coordinating all SS meetings and internals, member of UGC(Undergraduate Council)
  • Webmaster: Responsible for maintaining the SS website

Our work flow

Step 1: Issue Identification

We come to know about problems that are being faced by students through:

  • Listening to the student body
  • Suggestions from faculty and academics department
  • Suggestions from Senate members

Step 2: Issue Resolution

  • The identified issues are discussed in student senate
  • All senate members agree to particular solution/suggestions

Step 3: Approaching the Academic Department

  • The Coordinator of Student Senate now approaches the Academic Manager(Sheetu Ahuja) with the solutions/suggestions.
  • After that the Academic Manager approaches DoAA(Dean of Academic Affairs)
  • If the Senate is not able to come up with a solution regarding an issue, the Academic Manager is informed accordingly.
